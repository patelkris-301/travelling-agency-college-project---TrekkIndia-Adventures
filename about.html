About TrackIndia Adventures

TrackIndia Adventures is a specialist trekking and travel operator focused on curated, safe and memorable journeys across India — especially in Himachal Pradesh, Kashmir, Kerala, Meghalaya and Uttarakhand. Our approach blends local knowledge, experienced guides and clear package inclusions so you can focus on the adventure.

Our Story

Founded to create authentic trekking experiences, TrackIndia Adventures has grown by following best practices used by established agencies such as King Hills Travels and Invisible Tracking Agency. We started small and expanded responsibly: planning routes, building local partnerships, and improving safety procedures based on real field experience.

Operating Since: 2015
Over the years we have refined itineraries, logistics and customer support to deliver reliable trips for beginners and experienced trekkers alike.

Our Mission

To create thoughtfully planned, ethical and safe travel experiences that connect travelers to local communities and natural landscapes — while keeping pricing transparent and support reliable.

What We Offer (Services & Inclusions)

  • Clear itineraries: Day-by-day route plans with major waypoints and transfer details.
  • Meals: Lunch & dinner included on scheduled days (as commonly provided by agencies like King Hills Travels).
  • Transport: Point-to-point transfers (e.g., Delhi → Pathankot → Manali) as stated in each package.
  • Experienced local guides: Trained guides and support staff familiar with the region.
  • Permits & entry fees: Assistance with required local permits where applicable.
  • Accommodation: Hotel/guesthouse or basecamp stays as per package description.
  • Safety & first-aid: Basic first-aid kits, emergency contact procedures and risk-minimizing route choices.

Typical Package Coverage (reference-style)

Based on common inclusions seen with agencies such as King Hills Travels and Invisible Tracking Agency, our packages generally cover: coach or private transfers mentioned in the route, accommodation during the package, breakfast/lunch/dinner as indicated, guide fees, and group activities. Items usually NOT included: personal shopping, travel insurance, and optional activities where explicitly mentioned.
